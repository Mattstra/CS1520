The Chupacabra is a creature from South American folklore that is also known as

the goat sucker. The first supposed sightings of this creature were in Puerto

Rico during the mid 90s. The Chupacabra is most commonly described as being a

reptile like creature with spines running down its back. However there is also

another description of it looking more like a breed of dog with pronounced spines

running down its back.
