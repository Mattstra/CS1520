A cryptid is a creature whose existence has been suggested but not proven.

They often appear in folklore and myths like the Chupacabra and the Jersey Devil

Some of the most commonly known cryptids are Bigfoot, Loch Ness Monster, and the Chupacabra.

Cryptozoology is the pseudoscience dedicated to studying the existence of these creatures.
