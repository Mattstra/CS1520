The Loch Ness Monster is a creature from Scottish folklore that supposedly inhabits lake

Loch Ness. It is decribed as being a large aquatic creature with a long neck and several

humps. Many believe that the Loch Ness Monster is potentially from a line of dinosaurs

that have survived to this very day.
